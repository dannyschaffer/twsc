# **The Zenith Package**

_This hands-on executive coaching program ensures you will receive one-on-one support, proven resources, and coaching that The Wall Street Coach uses with **our most effective and productive executive leaders**. This personalized coaching system is dedicated to providing you with the highest level of clarity and planning so you can build a lifelong foundation for consistent, insightful, and lucrative career._

The Wall Street Coach is a coaching firm that specializes in helping executives hone their leadership skills and ensure leadership teams work more efficiently, effectively, and cohesively.

Our proven Zenith Package is tailor-made to your goals for your career. You will be working with a highly experienced coach who will help you understand how you’ve been stopping yourself and co-create a clear cut path so that you can finally realize your potential personally and professionally. Our curated team of experienced coaches have worked with C-suite executives, entry-level leaders, professionals and entrepreneurs looking for a new start, giving you the edge for realizing your goals for success.

**The Zenith Package is our most popular package because it ensures expert guidance and critical hard truth accountability to achieve the goals you’ve set for yourself and your career.**

## **Included in The Zenith Package:**

### **3 Month Effective Executive Coaching Program**

#### Coaching:

- Hand-selected placement with one of our elite coaches on The Wall Street Coach team.
- Two (2) sixty-minute coaching calls per month for three months.
- Email availability with your coach for urgent matters.
- An individualized coaching roadmap based on your future professional goals and your personal goals.
- Personalized support to hold you accountable.
- The empowering secret of self-responsibility.
- Tools provided on how to stay disciplined.
- Understand your current limited view, interpretation and position. And learn how to open up your view to the much grander vision.
- Finally stop feeling like you’re a cog in the machine.

#### Resources:

- **Discovery Packet** – Your personal Discovery Packet is a deep dive into how and where you have roadblocks. After you answer these questions you’ll be able to identify and pinpoint exactly where you are now and where you want to go and how to remove any roadblocks that stand in your way. Imagine finally knowing exactly what you need to do for your career – and your life – to finally breakthrough. Imagine being fully aware of all the places where you’re being stopped in your career and in your personal life. You’ll be shocked to discover how much your life mirrors your career. Answering these questions will jumpstart your coaching in unexpected ways.
- **Position Checklist** – Our custom-created checklist will help you identify exactly where you are emotionally & psychologically. This will provide you a cold hard truth on where you’re at before you ask for that raise, transition positions, or have a crucial conversation. This checklist is a game-changer.
- **Career Strategy Journal** – Downloadable journal complete with prompts to look at your career from a strategic perspective.
