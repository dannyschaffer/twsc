A Career Built on Integrity and Insight

I didn't start as a coach. I started in finance. For over a decade, I watched brilliant minds burn out. I saw "Masters of the Universe" who were financially rich but internally bankrupt.

In 2008, during the financial crisis, I realized the industry didn't need more algorithms. It needed a conscience. It needed leaders who understood that sustainable performance comes from sustainable psychology.

That realization changed everything. I dedicated myself to understanding not just how markets work, but how the human mind works under pressure. I studied the intersection of neuroscience, behavioral finance, and high-performance psychology.

Today, I work with traders, executives, and fund managers who want more than just profits. They want longevity. They want impact. They want to build something that lasts.
